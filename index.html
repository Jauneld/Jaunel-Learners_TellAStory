The Teddy Bear

It was a very hot day

So everyone ran to the mall to get cool

Harry, a plush dog loved when it is hot because the more people, the higher chances that he will get bought.

Harry was on the shelf waiting and watching sadden while people went by him.

The store was closing and Harry was sad that another day, he was not picked. That was when a little girl ran in the store with money.

The girl ran over the Harry and let out a sigh. "Finally, I have you" she said. Harry was happy that he was picked.
